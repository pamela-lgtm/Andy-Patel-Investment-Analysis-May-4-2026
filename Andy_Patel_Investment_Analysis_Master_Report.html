Scoring Methodology

The composite score weights the seven dimensions a cash long-term-hold investor should care about. All raw metrics normalized 0–100 within the set, then weighted as below.

Weight Distribution

AVM Gap (% under RealAVM, higher better)25%
Indicative Gross Yield (rent ÷ price)20%
Carrying Cost Efficiency (carry %, lower better)20%
Market Signals (DOM + reduction depth)15%
$/SqFt Value (relative to set, lower better)10%
AVM Confidence (Realist score)5%
Qualitative Flags (motivation, schools, layout, risk)5%

Important Disclosures

  • RentAVM is directional, not confirmed. Realist's rental estimates are MLS-derived. Verandah independent rental comp pull is recommended before any underwriting decision.
  • Property tax projected at 1.4% of purchase price. Current tax bills include homestead exemptions and assessment caps that will reset upon sale. This standardized projection ensures fair comparison across the set.
  • Flood insurance estimate ($1,500/yr placeholder) applied only to Town Lake Drive (Zone AE). Actual quotes vary $800–2,500 depending on elevation certificate and current NFIP/private market rates.
  • Carry-as-% excludes mortgage payments, property management fees, vacancy reserves, capex reserves, and insurance (other than flood where mandated). Fully-loaded yield will be lower than indicated.
  • This analysis assumes cash purchase and a long-term unfurnished rental hold strategy. Conclusions would shift meaningfully for short-term rental, fix-and-flip, or financed purchase scenarios.

Comparative Insights Across the Set

Patterns and standout data points from the eleven-property analysis.

Highest Indicative Yield

Citruswood Drive (Lake Nona) — 9.03% gross. The Lake Nona corridor's rental premium combined with a 4BR layout and motivated investor seller produces the strongest income profile in the set. Even at full ask, the math works.

Lowest Annual Carry

Stephs Field (Silver Springs REO) — 1.75% carry-as-%. No CDD, light HOA, no flood. Closely followed by Franklin (1.77%) and Jubiloso (1.79%). All three are detached SFR products — the format with the leanest ongoing cost structure.

Strongest Seller Distress

Franklin Road (Split Oak Reserve) — owner selling $24K below their own 2021 purchase price in an appreciating market. Out-of-state listing agent + vacant + recent 5.4% cut + closing credit + just 22 DOM into the cycle. Sunsail (#8) and Sunridge (#1) are close behind.

Largest AVM Discount

Sunridge Loop (Suncrest REO) — list is 7.7% under RealAVM with confidence 98. Bank-owned, three failed listings, just absorbed a $25K cut. A $290K cash offer would push the AVM gap above 14%.

Best Schools

Citruswood Drive and Town Lake Drive tie — both feed into Innovation High School with Innovation Middle. Lake Nona's premium feeder pattern. Sun Blaze Elementary serves Town Lake; Moss Park Elementary serves Citruswood.

Most Diagnostic Data Point

Sunsail Avenue's 7-month failed rental test — owner reduced rent seven times from $2,750 to $2,400 and never leased. RentAVM at $2,530 is unreliable for this property. Strongest single data point in the set for absorption-risk assessment.

Best Detached SFR Path

Three detached candidates: Stephs Field (REO, lowest carry, flex 4th BR), Franklin (strongest distress, conservative RentAVM), and Jubiloso (largest lot and footprint). Stephs Field offers the strongest investor economics; Franklin offers the best negotiation upside.

Biggest Risks to Avoid

Town Lake Drive (Zone AE flood + heaviest carry + zero motivation) and Sunsail Avenue (proven rental absorption problem). Both have surface appeal but structural issues that compound over a long-term hold horizon.

Recommended Next Steps for Andy

A phased path from this analysis to executed acquisition.

1Verandah Rental Comp Confirmation — Top 3 Properties

Before any offer is written, Verandah pulls independent rental comps within 0.5–1 mile of each top-3 property. Target: confirm RentAVM directionally for Sunridge, Citruswood, and Sunkissed. This converts directional yield numbers into underwritable income figures.

  • Sunridge: validate Suncrest 2BR townhouse rental velocity at $2,100–2,150
  • Citruswood: validate Lake Nona 4BR townhouse rental at $2,650–2,800 with 1st-floor primary premium
  • Sunkissed: already partially validated by sister-unit live ask at $2,150 — Verandah pulls supporting comps

2On-Site Walkthrough — Top 2 Priority Properties

Linda Lopez conducts walkthroughs of the top 2 ranked properties (Sunridge and Citruswood) with branded Verandah key packets. Focus areas:

  • Sunridge: condition assessment given REO "as-is" status, HVAC age, water heater, appliances, any signs of vandalism or deferred maintenance
  • Citruswood: 2012 build component-life review (HVAC, roof remaining life, water heater, appliances); rental marketability of 1-car garage limitation

3Offer Strategy Sequencing

Recommend offering on properties in priority order, not simultaneously. Allows leverage on each negotiation without overcommitting capital:

  • First offer — Sunridge at $290–295K cash, 30-day close (Fannie Mae HomePath, full proof of funds). Realistic counter range $300–308K. If accepted, AVM gap is +13–17%.
  • If Sunridge falls through — Citruswood at $342–348K cash, 30-day close. LLC investor seller responds to clean cash with proof of funds. Realistic counter range $350–355K. AVM gap stays at +5–7%.
  • Backup option — Sunkissed at $290–298K cash. Cleanest transaction, fastest close, lowest negotiation leverage but least friction.

4Pre-Offer Due Diligence Items

  • Sunridge / Stephs Field (REO): review HUD 24 CFR 206.125 disclosure requirements, RES.NET process, $300 buyer-side technology fee, ServiceLink title arrangements
  • Caspian (if pursued at AVM): tax shock post-sale (homestead drops), comps for Lakeshore at Narcoossee specifically given lower AVM confidence
  • Town Lake (if pursued): elevation certificate pull, NFIP and private flood insurance quotes, evaluate full carrying-cost impact at quoted premium
  • Sunsail (if pursued): deep rental absorption diagnostic — what specifically prevented leasing? Tour neighboring units, talk to property managers in the immediate area

5Verandah's Role Through Closing & Beyond

  • Rental comp confirmation and underwriting support
  • On-site property walkthroughs
  • Offer preparation guidance, condition contingency drafting
  • Post-close: lease-up marketing, tenant screening, full property management under Verandah's standard 8% management / 50% leasing fee structure
  • SureVestor Landlord Shield enrollment for GL coverage with no deductible (Verandah-managed properties only)
  • Experian rent reporting via AppFolio for tenant credit-building (key tenant retention tool)
